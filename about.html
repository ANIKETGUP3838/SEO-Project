About Budget Travel India Guide

Budget Travel India Guide was created to help students, young professionals, and families explore India and nearby countries without overspending. The goal is simple: travel more while spending less, through smart planning, budget stays, and local experiences.

This website focuses on practical advice: how to find cheap flights, where to book hostels and homestays, how to travel safely as a solo traveler, and how to design low-cost itineraries from major Indian cities. Every guide is written with a real Indian traveler in mind – INR budgets, realistic travel durations, and cost-saving hacks.

This site is also part of an academic project for the course CSET489 – Search Engine Optimization, demonstrating SEO best practices such as keyword optimization, internal linking, clean URL structure, mobile responsiveness, and performance-focused design.
